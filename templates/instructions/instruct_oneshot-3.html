Instructions

After you pass the quiz, you will type in your favorite question. It is a text-box, you will be allowed to ask anything you like.

After you type in a freeform question, you will be asked to rank 4 questions provided to you from best to worst. After you rank these 4 questions, you will get your top choice answered. This ranking is independent from your freeform response.

You will then get to choose your favorite object from a list of 20, as you did in the full game. Make sure you rank the questions well, because if you get the object correct you will recieve a bonus of $x
