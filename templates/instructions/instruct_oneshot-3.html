Instructions

After you pass the quiz on the initial questions and answers, you will be asked to type your ideal next question in a text box. You may ask anything you like.

After that, you will be asked to rank 6 possible next questions according to your preference from best to worst. Your top choice will be answered after you submit your ranking.

After selecting your preferences, you will be asked to guess the identity of the hidden object from a list of 20 arbitrary objects. If you rank the questions well, you will have a better chance of guessing the hidden object correctly. Correct responses are rewarded with a bonus of $0.20.
