Instructions

In this HIT, you will be presented with a series of 14 trials. In each of these trials there is a secret object, and it is your goal to guess this object. At the beginning of the trial, a series of yes or no questions and their corresponding answers will be shown to you. You are then asked to select just one more question from a list of possible questions. After your response you will be presented with a new trial, that has its own corresponding object and questions.
