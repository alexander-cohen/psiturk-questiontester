Instructions

Here is a more complete list of possible categories. However if you think it could be an object that fits into a category not on this list, that is still possible.

  • Animals
  • Insects
  • Plants
  • Food
  • Household items
  • Tools
  • Clothing
  • Modes of transportation
  • Sports
  • Objects
  • Other tangible items

Nothing in the following list will appear:

  • Specific people
  • Places
  • Proper nouns
  • Ideas
  • Verbs
  • Adjectives
  • Adverbs

In short, all 'things' are a possibility, and nothing else. There are only nouns in the list.
