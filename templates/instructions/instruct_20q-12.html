Instructions

When the game ends, you will be asked to guess the identity of the hidden object. Choose your best guess from a set of 20 options where only one choice is correct. The incorrect options are chosen at random from the set of 1000 possible objects. If you guess correctly, you will receive a bonus payment.
You may end the game any time you wish. Each question you ask decreases your potential bonus by $0.X. Thus, you are incentivized to seek information as efficiently as possible and to guess when you are reasonably certain you can pick out the right object from a set of 20 choices.
